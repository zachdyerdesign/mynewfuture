Following the Jesus Method

Jesus was a Hebrew. He employed a Hebrew (lamad) style of training, easily compared to coaching. The Hebrew language uses one root word (lamad) for the English words "to teach" and "to learn." So, only when the student is actually doing what the teacher taught, the teacher has succeeded.

Here teacher and follower connect information with implementation. Both are aware of the danger of "over-informing"—that information without implementation will not bring change. Jesus modeled this (lamad) approach with the expectation that we would follow it. It’s foundational to developing growth-centered relationships.
