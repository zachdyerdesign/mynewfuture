My Story

Wade Hankins

Wade is a Certified Transfor-mational Coach and Coach Train-er whose years of experience helps guide searching people who are looking for a better life.

His insights consistently unlock heart desires and shift people into creative planning and action.

Those who Wade has coached through change, get where they want to go. He helps others with visions of a new future. He has the unique ability to connect dreams and desires to action. All attest to Wade’s artful impact in personal transformation.

His experience includes start-ing Vineyard Christian Fellowship of Independence with John Wimber, and serving on the Board of Directors of Bridge Min-istries International with John Paul Jackson.

Wade has a passion for dream interpretation, and enjoys coach-ing others in their pursuit of hear-ing God.

Wade is married to Jan Hankins. They have four adult children. Wade and Jan live in Lee’s Summit, Missouri, at Lake Jacomo.
